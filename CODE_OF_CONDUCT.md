# Code of Conduct

## The short version
Be respectful. Disagreements happen — that's fine. Personal attacks, harassment, and discrimination are not.

## The longer version
This project is open to everyone regardless of experience level, background, or identity. Contributors are expected to:

- Be kind and constructive in discussions
- Accept feedback without taking it personally
- Give feedback without making it personal
- Assume good faith — most people are trying to help

The following will not be tolerated:

- Harassment or personal attacks of any kind
- Dismissing contributors because of their experience level
- Spam, trolling, or deliberately derailing discussions

## Reporting issues
If something happens that makes you uncomfortable, open a private issue or contact @creativemarsh directly.

This is a small project. The goal is to keep it a place where people actually want to contribute.
